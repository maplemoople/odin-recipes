Garlic Fried Rice

Garlic Fried Rice with fried egg and small sausages

Simple filipino breakfast fried rice. Fairly quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Day Old Rice
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Butter
  • Sausage of choice
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Ajinomoto MSG (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut a full garlic clove into tiny garlic chunks
  2. Throw butter and garlic in pan around medium heat
  3. Wait till garlic is browned to your liking
  4. Add day old rice making sure its not in huge chunks
  5. Add some salt and a little MSG
  6. To cook your sausage, Put your sausage in a pan with high heat
  7. Once hot, put a little water to boil the sausage and let the water boil away
  8. Let the sausage now cook in its own oil till it sears well
  9. Now serve with an Over Easy or Sunny Side Up egg and enjoy :)
