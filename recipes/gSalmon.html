Garlic Salmon

Broiled salmon with garlic mayo sauce

My go to recipe for making salmon that is quick, easy, and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Garlic Paste
  • Salmon
  • Mayo
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Start by mixing the garlic paste with mayo
  2. Add the amount of salt and pepper to your liking to the garlic mayo
  3. Put your salmon on a tray
  4. Spread your garlic mayo onto the top of your salmon completely covering it
  5. Broil salmon for around 10 minutes or until your desired
  6. Serve ontop of a nice bed of rice and a side of a vegetable of choice then enjoy :)
