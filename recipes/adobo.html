Mom's Adobo

Pork Adobo with rice and green onions

A salty sour savory pork dish straight from my mom. My personal favorite Filipino dish that is super easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder
  • White Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Bay Leaves
  • Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut Pork into roughly 1 inch cubes and place into pot
  2. Fill pot a bit more than half of the height of the pork with water
  3. Fill the rest of the height of the pork with vinegar
  4. Add 2 Bay Leaves and a lot of Grinded Black Pepper (and whole peppercorns if you want)
  5. Heat up to a boil and simmer for roughly 40 minutes
  6. Add a bit of soy sauce (add a little less than you would think, you can always add more after)
  7. Let it simmer for another 20 or until the pork is super soft

    8. Optional

    7.5. Take the pork out and fry them with oil on a seperate pan till the sides are nice and seared

  8. Now serve with some white rice and add a bit of green onions and other toppings to enjoy :)
